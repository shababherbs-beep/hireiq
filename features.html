AI Candidate Scoring

Every CV gets a precise match score from 0–100 based on how well the candidate aligns with your job requirements. The AI evaluates skills, experience, education, and more — with clear reasons for every score.

  • 0–100 match score for every candidate
  • Detailed reasons explaining the score
  • Skills gap analysis per candidate
  • Red flag automatic detection
  • Custom scoring weights per requirement

Built for Teams

HireIQ is designed for HR teams, not just individuals. Multiple team members can access the same screening results, add notes, approve or reject candidates, and collaborate in real time.

  • Multiple user accounts per organization
  • Role-based access (Admin, Reviewer, Viewer)
  • Comment and note on individual candidates
  • Approve/reject workflow for shortlisting
  • Activity log — see who did what and when
