How It Works

From job description to
ranked shortlist in minutes

No training required. No IT setup. Just upload and let AI do the screening.

Step by Step
4 simple steps to smarter hiring
01
📋
Paste your job description
Copy and paste your job requirements into HireIQ. Include the role title, required skills, experience level, and any specific qualifications. The more detail you provide, the more accurate the AI screening will be.
💡 Tip: Include both "must have" and "nice to have" requirements. HireIQ will weight them accordingly.
02
📤
Upload your CVs in bulk
Upload up to 100 CVs at once — PDF or Word format both supported. HireIQ reads every CV automatically, extracting skills, experience, education, and achievements with high accuracy.
💡 Tip: You can drag and drop a whole folder of CVs at once. No need to rename or organize them first.
03
🤖
AI screens and scores every candidate
Our AI reads each CV against your job description and assigns a match score from 0–100. It evaluates skills match, years of experience, education, and career progression. The entire process takes under 2 minutes for 100 CVs.
💡 Tip: The AI also flags red flags like employment gaps or mismatched experience levels automatically.
04
🏆
Review ranked shortlist & share
Receive a ranked list of all candidates with their AI score, a summary of strengths and weaknesses, and key highlights from their CV. Export as PDF or share a branded report link with your hiring team.
💡 Tip: Use the "Notes" feature to add your own comments to each candidate before sharing with your team.

FAQ
Frequently asked questions
How accurate is the AI screening? +
HireIQ achieves 98% accuracy in matching candidates to job requirements. Our AI is trained on millions of CVs and job descriptions across industries. However, we always recommend using HireIQ as a tool to assist your decision, not replace it.
What file formats are supported? +
HireIQ supports PDF and Microsoft Word (.doc and .docx) formats. These cover 99% of CVs received by HR teams. We're working on adding image-based CV support as well.
How long does screening take? +
HireIQ processes 100 CVs in under 2 minutes. The exact time depends on CV length and complexity, but you'll rarely wait more than 3 minutes even for the largest batches.
Is my data safe and private? +
Absolutely. All CV data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never share or sell your data. You can delete all data at any time. HireIQ is fully GDPR compliant and designed with enterprise data security standards.
Can I use HireIQ for multiple job roles at once? +
Yes! On the Pro and Enterprise plans, you can run multiple screening sessions simultaneously — one per job role. Each session has its own job description, CV pool, and ranked shortlist.
Do I need any technical skills to use HireIQ? +
None at all. HireIQ is designed for HR professionals, not IT teams. If you can use email and upload a file, you can use HireIQ. No installation, no configuration, no training required.
