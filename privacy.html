1. Overview

🔒 Your privacy matters to us. HireIQ is built with data privacy at its core. We collect only what we need, store it securely, and never sell your data.

This Privacy Policy explains how Autremix ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use HireIQ ("the Service"). By using HireIQ, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

HireIQ is operated by Autremix, founded by Mohsin Munir, based in Faisalabad, Pakistan. We serve enterprise customers globally and are committed to maintaining the highest standards of data privacy and security.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Account Information

  • Full name and email address
  • Company name, size, and industry
  • Job title and role
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Password (stored encrypted, never in plain text)

Usage Data

  • Pages visited and features used
  • Number of CVs processed
  • Login times and session duration
  • Browser type, device, and IP address

Payment Information

  • Billing name and address
  • Payment is processed by Stripe — we never store full card numbers

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data strictly to provide and improve HireIQ. Specifically:

  • To create and manage your account
  • To process CV screening requests using AI
  • To generate reports and shortlists
  • To send service-related emails (account, billing, security)
  • To improve our AI models and product features
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not use your data for:

  • Selling or renting to third parties
  • Targeted advertising
  • Training general-purpose AI models outside of HireIQ
  • Any purpose not listed above without your explicit consent

4. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored on secure cloud servers with the following protections:

  • Encryption in transit: All data transferred between your browser and our servers uses TLS 1.3 encryption
  • Encryption at rest: All stored data is encrypted using AES-256
  • Access controls: Strict role-based access — only authorized personnel can access your data
  • Backups: Daily encrypted backups with 30-day retention
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring and intrusion detection

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all your data is permanently deleted within 30 days.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data only with:

  • Stripe: Payment processing (subject to Stripe's privacy policy)
  • OpenAI: CV text is sent to OpenAI's API for AI analysis. OpenAI does not use API data for model training
  • Supabase: Secure database hosting
  • Legal authorities: Only if required by law or court order

All third-party providers are vetted for security and privacy compliance before integration.

6. CV & Candidate Data

⚠️ Important: You are responsible for ensuring you have the right to upload and process any CVs you submit to HireIQ. Always obtain proper consent from candidates before processing their data.

When you upload CVs to HireIQ:

  • CV content is transmitted securely to our AI processing system
  • CV data is stored temporarily during the screening session
  • You can delete CV data at any time from your dashboard
  • CV data is automatically deleted after 90 days of inactivity
  • We do not access or read CV content except for AI processing purposes

As a data controller, you are responsible for candidate data compliance in your jurisdiction. HireIQ acts as a data processor on your behalf.

7. Cookies

HireIQ uses the following cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for login sessions and security — cannot be disabled
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how the product is used (can be disabled)
  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings and preferences

You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect product functionality.

8. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to specific uses of your data
  • Restriction: Request restriction of data processing

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@autremix.com. We respond to all requests within 30 days.

9. GDPR Compliance

HireIQ is designed to be GDPR compliant. Our legal basis for processing personal data includes:

  • Contract: Processing necessary to provide the service you signed up for
  • Legitimate interests: Product improvement and security monitoring
  • Legal obligation: Compliance with applicable laws
  • Consent: Marketing communications (you can opt out anytime)

For EU/EEA customers, data is processed in accordance with GDPR. We offer Data Processing Agreements (DPA) for Enterprise customers upon request.

10. Children's Privacy

HireIQ is a professional business service intended for use by adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at privacy@autremix.com.

11. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page
  • Send an email notification to all registered users
  • Display a notice in the HireIQ dashboard

Continued use of HireIQ after policy changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
