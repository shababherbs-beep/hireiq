FAQ
Pricing questions answered
Is there a free trial?+
Yes! All plans include full access to Pro features. No credit card is required to start, and you only pay when you choose to continue.
Can I change my plan later?+
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing period.
What happens if I go over my CV limit?+
On the Starter plan, you'll be notified when you reach 50 CVs and can upgrade to continue. We never cut off access mid-screening — we'll let you finish and then prompt you to upgrade.
Do you offer discounts for Pakistani companies?+
Yes! We offer special pricing for Pakistani companies and startups. Contact us at hello@hireiq.com to discuss. We also offer NGO and educational institution discounts.
How does annual billing work?+
Annual billing is charged once per year and gives you 20% off compared to monthly billing. You can still cancel anytime and receive a prorated refund for unused months.
What payment methods are accepted?+
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) via Stripe. For Enterprise customers, we also support bank transfer and invoice-based billing.
